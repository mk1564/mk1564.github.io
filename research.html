Working paper

Re-estimating Potential GDP: New Evidence on Output Hysteresis (with Diego Anzoategui)
ðŸ’¡

This paper provides a structural estimation method for potential GDP. The method has advantages over existing methods of being simple and immune to Lucas Critique. Applied to US data, it shows that demand shocks have persistent (but not permanent) effects on potential GDP.

Publication

Fair international protocols for the abatement of GHG emissions (with Biung-Ghi Ju, Suyi Kim, and Juan D. Moreno-Ternero), Energy Economics, 2021
ðŸ’¡

We lay out theoretical framework for green house gas emission allocation problems in which four allocation rules are characterized in historical and fairness perspective. We compute CO2 emissions using those rules and compare them with the allocation proposed by the Kyoto Protocol.
