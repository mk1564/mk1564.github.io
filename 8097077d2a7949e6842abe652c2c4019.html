Re-estimating Potential GDP: New Evidence on Output Hysteresis with Diego Anzoategui

This paper provides a structural estimation method for potential GDP, which has a practical advantage of being simple and easy-to-use. By applying the method to US data, the paper shows that demand shocks have persistent but not permanent effects on potential GDP.
